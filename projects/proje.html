Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune
to the fish eating anemone's leather sting.

Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
Average Length:11 centimeters
Average Lifespan:7 years,
Habitat:Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive whitebars,clown
anemonefish are among the most recognisable of all reff-
dwellers.They reach about 11 centimeters in length,and
are named for multicolored sea anemone in which
they make their homes.
